New paper: [Open Technical Problems in Open-Weight AI Model Risk Management](https://papers.ssrn.com/sol3/papers.cfm?abstract_id=5705186). We present 16 open technical challenges spanning training, evaluation, deployment, and ecosystem monitoring, emphasizing that openness about research and methods—not just weights—is key to rigorous risk management.
