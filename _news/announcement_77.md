Our paper [AI for Scientific Discovery is a Social Problem](https://www.cell.com/patterns/fulltext/S2666-3899(26)00006-1) is now out in Patterns. This piece, with Georgia Channing, pushes back on the idea that AI for science is just a matter of scaling up models and optimization loops. A lot of the real difficulty is social, institutional, and deeply tied to how science actually gets done.
