Our paper [AI for Scientific Discovery is a Social Problem](https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S2666389926000061) is now out in Patterns. This piece, with Georgia Channing, pushes back on the idea that AI for science is just a matter of scaling up models and optimization loops. A lot of the real difficulty is social, institutional, and deeply tied to how science actually gets done.
