New paper: [Economies of Open Intelligence](https://www.dataprovenance.org/economies-of-open-intelligence.pdf), covered by the [Financial Times](https://www.ft.com/content/931c8218-a9d7-4cbd-8b08-27516637ff41). Using 2.2B model downloads across 851k models, we show how global power in open AI has shifted dramatically: Chinese developers now surpass the U.S. in adoption, while community developers have grown from 30% to over 60% of the ecosystem.
